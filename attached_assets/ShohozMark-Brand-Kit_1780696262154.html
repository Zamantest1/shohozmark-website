09 — Brand Rules
Do's & Don'ts
✓ Do
Always use Growth Green (#00C853) as the primary brand color
Use Syne font for all headings and logo text
Keep the logo icon and text together whenever possible
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the icon height
Use the tagline "Marketing Made Easy" consistently
Keep designs clean and minimal — let the green do the talking
✗ Don't
Never stretch or distort the logo
Never use low contrast colors that reduce readability
Never place logo on busy or cluttered backgrounds
Never use random fonts — stick to Syne and DM Sans only
Never change the brand green to any other shade
Never use the icon without the wordmark in formal documents
